## What Toggle Actually Is

Toggle is not a marketing agency. It's a growth partner.

The distinction: most agencies diagnose a symptom (not enough leads, low ROAS) and sell a solution (run more ads). Toggle diagnoses the system first — before recommending a single channel or spending a dollar on creative.

They benchmark metrics **outside marketing scope** before recommending anything:
- Retail footfall conversion rates
- Sales team first-response time (FRT) on inbound leads
- Website bounce rates on lead form pages
- CRM workflow gaps

These reveal whether the bottleneck is product, process, or UI — before anyone touches ad creative.

**The retainer model's structural flaw:** It requires agencies to sell marketing regardless of client readiness. That's why SMEs keep asking why their marketing isn't working. The diagnosis was wrong from the start.

Toggle's position: we tell you the obstacle before selling the solution. Sometimes that means referring to a partner for website UI. Sometimes it means fixing the sales cycle before spending a dollar on ads.

## The Problem They Solve

Modern brands face 3 challenges:
1. **Wrong diagnosis** — told they need more ads when the real problem is the website, sales process, or CRM
2. **Too many moving parts** — marketing, CRM, SEO, website, sales all disconnected
3. **Following average strategy** — industry average produces average results

Toggle solves this by looking at the entire growth system, not just the ad account.
