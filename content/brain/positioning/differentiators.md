# Differentiators

**Purpose.** The five reasons Toggle wins. Cited by proposals, landing pages, sales decks. Each is a claim Toggle can defend with evidence (case study, process artefact, named operator).

## The five

1. **We focus on what drives profit.** Not vanity metrics, not impressions for their own sake — the work is judged on revenue, qualified pipeline, and cost-to-acquire.
2. **Senior operators only.** Every practice is led by senior operators who work directly with clients. No junior account-manager layer between the strategy and the execution.
3. **Everything under one partner.** Acquisition, conversion, and retention run on the same team, on the same data, with the same accountability — instead of stitched-together specialists.
4. **Cross-industry thinking.** Patterns that work in higher-ed get tested in insurance; patterns from e-commerce get applied to banking. Pattern transfer is a feature, not a coincidence.
5. **Long-term growth, not short-term wins.** Engagements are built around compounding gains — authority, infrastructure, creative libraries, retention systems — not one-quarter spikes that collapse the next.
