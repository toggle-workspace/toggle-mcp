## What It Is

Toggle Bespoke is Toggle's non-retainer, premium advisory arm.

The core product: **a one-weekend intensive** where senior marketers work hands-on with the client's team to build, launch, and document a paid marketing system — so the client can run it confidently without an agency.

The transformation:
- **Before:** Stuck guessing. Relying on external agencies with no plan to bring capabilities in-house. Unsure if tracking, pixels, and campaigns are set up correctly. Paying for advice the team can't use.
- **After:** Ready to execute. Own your data, ad accounts, and strategy — forever. No more renting success. Fully built, audited, and documented by senior professionals.

---

## The One-Weekend Model

In one weekend, Toggle Bespoke:
1. **Tells you what to run** — analyzes business, audience, and goals to identify exact channels and campaigns
2. **Builds it with your team** — sets up campaigns, targeting, creatives, and tracking live, with the team learning alongside
3. **Shows you how to manage it** — delivers documentation, dashboards, and 30 days of support
