# Kualesa

_E-commerce · ASEAN Expansion_

## Challenge

Seeking a performance marketing partner to improve ROAS and lower CAC after struggling with high Meta ad spend, a lack of channel diversification, and stagnant growth within the ASEAN region.

## Solution

Implemented a "test-before-scale" strategy by lowering initial ad spend to focus on creative testing and localised visual angles. Once successful angles were identified, spend was diversified across Google, TikTok, and Meta (utilising Advantage+ Shopping campaigns) while expanding market reach into Singapore, Brunei, Philippines, Dubai, and other ASEAN countries.

## Results

| Metric                          | Result                                |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| ROAS increase                   | +45%                                  |
| Stable ROAS maintained at scale | 2.0x                                  |
| New markets entered             | Singapore, Brunei, Philippines, Dubai |
