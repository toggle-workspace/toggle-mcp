# UNITAR International University

_Education · Malaysia_

**From Moonshot to Milestone: Redefining UNITAR's 40k Lead Acquisition**

## Overview

Established in 1997 as Southeast Asia's first virtual university, UNITAR International University is a pioneer in digital-first education and the first institution in Asia to earn a QS 5-Star Rating for Online Learning. With a focus on accessibility and employability, the university maintains a 98% graduate employment rate by delivering affordable, MQA-accredited programs through its main campus and 13 regional centers across Malaysia.

## Challenge

UNITAR needed a sustainable lead generation engine to fuel aggressive enrollment targets, deliver high volumes of qualified leads, reach prospects across all locations in Malaysia, and keep acquisition costs well below industry benchmarks.

By mid-2024, their campaigns faced rising CPLs (Google RM154, FB RM291, TikTok RM525) and inconsistent media performance. Despite significant investments, campaigns weren't scaling efficiently, requiring a complete re-engineering of their marketing strategy.

## Solution

Toggle implemented a full-funnel performance marketing strategy combining precision targeting, creative storytelling, and disciplined media operations.

**#1. Marketing Messaging Framework**

A structured messaging playbook aligned to the student decision journey:

- Top of Funnel (TOF): Myth-busting, aspirational, curiosity-driven content ("Is Traditional Education Holding You Back?", "Does University Prestige Really Matter?")
- Middle of Funnel: Reassurance messaging highlighting UNITAR's unique value proposition ("UNITAR: Designed for the Real World", "Learn, Apply, Get Hired")
- Bottom of Funnel: Urgency and conversion-driven CTAs ("One Click Away — Complete Your Application", "Don't Let This Opportunity Slip Away")

**#2. Media Planning & Channel Strategy**

- Google SEM + PMax → high-intent leads (students actively searching for degrees/universities)
- Meta (Facebook/Instagram) → scalable mid-funnel engagement using creative storytelling and lead forms, boosted by campaign consolidation for stronger learning signals
- TikTok → TOF and awareness building with strong emotional/relatable hooks, designed to shift perceptions among younger audiences

**#3. Platform Hygiene & Optimisation**

- Facebook CAPI (Conversions API): server-side conversion signals for stronger attribution and improved campaign learning
- Campaign Consolidation: reduced fragmentation across Meta campaigns, improving delivery efficiency and reducing audience overlap
- Audience Targeting Optimisation: shifted from broad, inefficient targeting to layered intent + lookalike audiences
- Spend Pacing Controls: smarter daily pacing avoided budget burn while scaling spend past RM11 million/month at stable CPLs

**Services delivered:** Performance Marketing · Creative Production · Reporting & Analysis · Website Tracking

## Results

| Metric                                                   | Result                |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Qualified leads generated                                | 32,000+               |
| CPL reduction YoY                                        | 47%                   |
| Market impression share (Education & Training, Malaysia) | 77%                   |
| Period                                                   | June – September 2025 |

## Key insight

A full-funnel messaging matrix (TOF to A3) is essential for bridging the gap between curiosity-driven leads and high-intent conversions. Channel diversification — leveraging Google for immediate demand, Meta for nurturing, and TikTok for future demand — created a sustainable ecosystem for massive scaling. The synergy between continuous creative rotation and a shift toward layered audience intent proved to be the primary driver in surpassing the most ambitious enrollment targets.
