# Great Eastern Singapore

_Insurance · Financial Services · Singapore_

## Overview

Great Eastern Singapore is one of the region's leading insurance providers, offering a comprehensive range of life, health, and general insurance products. While the brand has strong recognition, maintaining dominance in organic search is critical for capturing high-intent users actively researching insurance plans online.

## Challenge

Despite strong brand presence, Great Eastern faced several SEO challenges:

- Key product-related search terms such as "insurance plan" were underperforming
- Average keyword rankings were stagnating around position #4
- Meta titles and descriptions lacked optimised keyword targeting and semantic depth
- Search snippets were not fully optimised for CTR, reducing potential organic traffic gains
- Competitors were aggressively optimising their SEO strategies

## Solution

Toggle implemented a focused, data-driven on-page SEO optimisation strategy:

**#1. Metadata Optimisation** — Refreshed title tags to improve keyword prominence and alignment with search intent. Rewrote meta descriptions to enhance clarity, keyword density, and CTR appeal. Ensured semantic alignment between metadata and on-page content.

**#2. Keyword-Content Alignment** — Enhanced keyword-to-content relationships across priority landing pages. Improved internal keyword structuring to reinforce topical authority. Optimised headings and content hierarchy to improve crawlability and context signals.

**#3. Search Relevance & SERP Performance** — Strengthened semantic keyword coverage to improve ranking potential. Optimised snippets to better match user search intent. Focused on high-impact product category pages with the greatest commercial value.

**Services delivered:** On-Page SEO Optimisation · Metadata Restructuring and Enhancement · SERP Performance Optimisation · Content Keyword Refinement · Ongoing Performance Tracking and Ranking Analysis

## Results

| Metric                              | Result   |
| ----------------------------------- | -------- |
| Average keyword ranking improvement | #4 → #1  |
| Organic clicks increase             | +16%     |
| Period                              | 2 months |

## Key insight

Small but strategic on-page optimisations can drive significant ranking improvements when focused on high-value keywords. Well-structured metadata plays a critical role in both search visibility and click-through performance. In highly competitive industries like insurance, even incremental SEO gains translate into meaningful business impact.
