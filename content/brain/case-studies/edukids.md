# EduKids

_E-commerce · Education · Malaysia_

## Challenge

Struggling to exceed a 4x ROAS on TikTok (target was 6x) while facing minimal sales on a newly launched website, which hindered the brand's ability to scale effectively and meet revenue goals.

## Solution

Refined audience targeting and ad messaging to resonate deeply with ideal customers. Key tactics included A/B testing on TikTok to reach high-purchase-intent audiences and leveraging Meta Ads with landing page optimisation to drive initial website revenue.

## Results

| Metric                  | Result                     |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------- |
| TikTok ROAS achieved    | 6x (target met by month 2) |
| Monthly website revenue | RM14,000                   |
| Conversion rate         | 5.5% (consistent)          |
