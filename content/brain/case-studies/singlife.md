# Singlife

_Insurance · Financial Services · Singapore (YMYL)_

## Overview

Singlife is a leading digital-first insurance provider in Singapore, offering life, health, and investment-linked insurance solutions. Operating in a YMYL (Your Money Your Life) category, content quality and authority signals play a decisive role in organic search rankings.

## Challenge

Despite strong brand presence, Singlife faced several organic search challenges:

- Core insurance keywords ranking around position #3, limiting maximum visibility and click capture
- Competing financial institutions strengthening long-form, authoritative content to dominate top positions
- Content required deeper alignment with user intent
- E.E.A.T. (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) signals needed enhancement to meet evolving Google algorithm standards for YMYL industries
- Competitive differentiation in search results was increasingly difficult

## Solution

Toggle implemented a comprehensive on-page content optimisation strategy centred on authority building, intent alignment, and structured relevance:

**#1. Intent-Aligned Content Expansion** — Developed keyword-rich, semantically structured content aligned with high-intent search queries. Expanded topical coverage to address informational, comparison-based, and transactional intent. Improved depth and comprehensiveness of priority insurance pages to outperform competing content.

**#2. Strengthening E.E.A.T. Signals** — Enhanced credibility markers within content to reinforce expertise and trustworthiness. Improved clarity, factual accuracy, and structured explanations of insurance-related topics. Optimised content presentation to align with Google's expectations for YMYL industries.

**#3. Structural & Relevance Optimisation** — Refined heading structures (H1–H3) to improve semantic hierarchy and crawlability. Improved internal linking to reinforce topical authority and contextual relationships. Optimised on-page elements to strengthen keyword prominence without over-optimisation.

**Services delivered:** Comprehensive On-Page Content Optimisation · Content Structuring and Authority Reinforcement · Keyword Expansion and Semantic Enhancement · E.E.A.T. Signal Strengthening · Ongoing Ranking and Performance Analysis

## Results

| Metric                              | Result   |
| ----------------------------------- | -------- |
| Average keyword ranking improvement | #3 → #1  |
| Organic clicks increase             | +23%     |
| Period                              | 3 months |

## Key insight

In YMYL industries, authority and trust signals are just as important as keyword optimisation. Comprehensive, intent-driven content consistently outperforms shallow optimisation tactics. Even a ranking improvement from #3 to #1 can generate substantial traffic gains in competitive verticals. A well-structured content hierarchy and strong semantic alignment improve both search engine crawlability and overall user experience.
