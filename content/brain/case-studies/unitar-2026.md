## Case Study 1: UNITAR International University
**Sub-title:** From Moonshot to Milestone: Redefining UNITAR's 40k Lead Acquisition

### Client
Southeast Asia's first virtual university (est. 1997). First institution in Asia to earn QS 5-Star Rating for Online Learning. 98% graduate employment rate. 13 regional centers across Malaysia.

### Problem
Saturated education market. Rising CPLs by mid-2024:
- Google: RM154 CPL
- Facebook: RM291 CPL
- TikTok: RM525 CPL

Campaigns weren't scaling efficiently despite significant investment. Needed complete re-engineering of marketing strategy to hit aggressive enrollment targets.

### Toggle's Strategy
Full-funnel performance marketing combining precision targeting, creative storytelling, and disciplined media operations.

1. **Marketing Messaging Framework** — Structured playbook aligned to student decision journey:
   - TOF: Myth-busting, aspirational content ("Is Traditional Education Holding You Back?")
   - MOF: Reassurance messaging ("UNITAR: Designed for the Real World", "Learn, Apply, Get Hired")
   - BOF: Urgency CTAs ("One Click Away—Complete Your Application")

2. **Channel Strategy** — Platform roles defined:
   - Google SEM + PMax → High-intent leads (actively searching)
   - Meta → Mid-funnel engagement, lead forms, campaign consolidation
   - TikTok → TOF awareness, emotional hooks, shifting perception with younger audiences

3. **Platform Hygiene & Technical Optimization:**
   - Facebook CAPI for server-side conversion signals
   - Campaign consolidation to reduce audience overlap
   - Shifted from broad to layered intent + lookalike audiences
   - Spend pacing controls to scale past RM11M/month without CPL inflation

### Results (June–September 2025)
- **32,000+ qualified leads** generated
- **47% reduction in CPL** year-on-year
- **77% of Total Impressions** captured in Malaysia's Education & Training category

### Key Insight
Scaling smarter through technical precision and relevant messaging can achieve what was once considered impossible. Channel diversification — with distinct roles per platform — allows massive scale without sacrificing efficiency.

## Case Study 2: Al Hidayah Publications
**Sub-title:** From Plateau to Profit: Scaling Al Hidayah's TikTok Shop through GMV Max Precision

### Client
Prominent Malaysian publisher specializing in Islamic literature — Quranic texts, Tafsir, educational and motivational books. Trusted source for the local Muslim community.

### Problem
Plateau in TikTok Shop sales. Organic reach and standard "Purchase" campaigns were no longer driving incremental growth. Needed to break stagnation, boost total orders and revenue, while maintaining a high, sustainable ROI.

### Toggle's Strategy
Moved away from traditional campaign structures to a **tiered TikTok GMV Max system**:
- **Scale Up tier** — Best-sellers with high budgets to maximize revenue
- **Maintain tier** — Mid-tier products for testing
- **Scale Down tier** — Low-performing stock cleared with minimal spend

Paired with an **affiliate creator network** to:
- Feed the GMV Max algorithm with organic-style, high-converting content
- Reduce cost of customer acquisition
- Leverage TikTok's community-first nature (authentic reviews beat polished commercials)

Developed tier-specific creative content focusing on emotional and educational benefits of books.

### Results (4-month period)
- **11.50 ROI** (consistent, representing 5% efficiency increase)
- **300% surge in total revenue**
- **200% improvement in overall orders**

### Key Insight
For TikTok Shop, the tiering system's waste reduction is as important as scaling winners. Cutting budget from low-performers freed capital to triple revenue from best-sellers. Creator-led content outperforms brand-only content on TikTok's algorithm.

## Case Study 3: Kualesa Co.
**Sub-title:** Saving ROAS for a Premium Polo Shirt Brand

### Client
Premium polo shirt brand targeting the ASEAN market.

### Problem
Struggling with high Meta ad spend concentration, lack of channel diversification, and stagnant growth. Needed a performance partner to improve ROAS and lower CAC after hitting a growth ceiling.

### Toggle's Strategy
**"Test-before-scale" methodology:**
1. Lowered initial ad spend to focus on creative testing and localized visual angles
2. Identified winning creative angles per market
3. Diversified spend across Google, TikTok, and Meta (Advantage+ Shopping campaigns)
4. Expanded market reach into Singapore, Brunei, Philippines, Dubai, and other ASEAN countries

### Results
- **+45% increase in ROAS**
- **Stable 2.0 ROAS** maintained while scaling internationally
- **New market presence** established in Singapore and Brunei

### Key Insight
Creative testing is not a phase — it's the foundation. Localized visual angles per market unlock international scale that a single creative approach never could.

## Case Study 4: EduKids Books
**Sub-title:** Unlocking Growth: EduKid Books' Digital Transformation with Toggle

### Client
Children's educational book brand launching a new e-commerce website.

### Problem

## Case Study 5: Kith & Kin Realty
**Sub-title:** How a Real Estate Agency Increased Lead Volume by 392%

### Client
Real estate agency in Kuala Lumpur and Selangor.

### Problem
Over-reliance on inconsistent organic methods and traditional cold-calling to generate leads for property developments. No scalable digital lead generation system.

### Toggle's Strategy
Launched targeted Meta Ad campaigns with:
- Strategic mix of high-intent lead generation formats
- Message-specific creatives (GIFs, images, videos)
- Targeting less competitive market gaps rather than fighting in the most saturated placements

### Results (3-month period)
- **392% increase in leads**
- **CPL of RM35** (maintained)
- **10% conversion rate** from leads to closed deals

### Key Insight
Real estate lead generation doesn't require the most spend — it requires finding underserved demand gaps and showing up there with the right message format.

## Case Study 6: TPL Fresh Meats
**Sub-title:** Scaling to RM200k Revenue with Reused Organic Content

### Client
Fresh meats retailer with strong local walk-in presence in Bangsar and Damansara communities. Transitioning to online sales.

### Problem
Needed to establish consistent online sales while expanding brand reach. Competitive fresh food market. Already had strong local brand but no digital revenue engine.

### Toggle's Strategy
**Organic asset mining + creative amplification:**
1. Deep audit of TPL's social media history to identify "winner" posts (high engagement, organic sales)
2. Transformed top-performing organic content into paid ad units — leveraging proven authenticity to bypass "ad blindness"
3. Produced new high-fidelity creative based on insights from winning posts
4. Dual-platform: Meta (visual appeal of fresh meat selection) + Google (high-intent local delivery searches)
5. Conversion funnel built directly to "ready to eat" and "imported selection" pages — removed friction from the buying process

### Results
- **RM200,000 in revenue**
- **20x+ ROAS**
- **1,400+ individual sales transactions**

### Key Insight
For trusted local retailers, organic content that already converts is the most efficient creative asset. Polished studio ads underperform authentic community-tested content. A small percentage of organic posts drive the majority of conversions — identifying and funding those "gold assets" is the fastest path to scale.
