# CIMB Malaysia

_Banking · Financial Services · Malaysia (YMYL)_

## Overview

CIMB Malaysia is one of the region's leading banking institutions, offering retail banking, credit cards, loans, wealth management, and corporate financial services. As a major financial brand within a YMYL category, search engine visibility plays a crucial role in customer acquisition, product awareness, and long-term brand authority.

## Challenge

Despite strong brand presence, CIMB faced several SEO challenges:

- Product and category pages underperforming due to limited keyword depth and insufficient semantic coverage
- Lack of strong topical authority reducing competitiveness for high-value financial keywords
- Existing blog content contained redundancies and outdated material, diluting overall content quality signals
- Internal approval processes within a large financial institution slowed content publishing cycles
- Delayed deployment reduced the speed of SEO impact in a fast-moving, competitive banking landscape

## Solution

Toggle repositioned the SEO strategy toward a content-led authority model:

**#1. Systematic Content Pruning** — Conducted a comprehensive audit of existing blog content. Removed, consolidated, or optimised redundant and underperforming articles. Improved overall content quality signals to strengthen domain authority.

**#2. High-Intent Content Development** — Identified keyword gaps across priority banking and financial topics. Developed high-intent, keyword-focused articles targeting informational and commercial queries. Strengthened semantic keyword coverage to improve ranking potential.

**#3. Strengthening Topical Authority** — Aligned content creation with CIMB's core financial product pillars. Built structured internal linking frameworks to reinforce content clusters. Focused on long-term authority building rather than isolated keyword wins.

**#4. Workflow & Stakeholder Collaboration** — Collaborated closely with internal teams to streamline approval processes. Established clearer publishing workflows to reduce deployment delays. Prioritised high-impact financial topics to accelerate measurable SEO gains.

**Services delivered:** Strategic Content Audit and Blog Pruning · Keyword Gap Analysis and Authority Mapping · Development of High-Intent Financial Articles · Internal Linking and Content Clustering Optimisation · Cross-Functional Collaboration to Improve Publishing Efficiency · Ongoing Performance Monitoring and SEO Refinement

## Results

| Metric                        | Result                                            |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Organic impressions increase  | +52.6%                                            |
| Organic clicks growth         | +34.5%                                            |
| Keyword position improvements | Double-digit gains across multiple priority terms |
| Period                        | 12 months                                         |

## Key insight

In highly competitive financial sectors, building topical authority is more impactful than isolated keyword optimisation. Systematic blog pruning can significantly enhance overall site quality signals and ranking potential. Cross-functional collaboration is critical when working within large organisations with complex approval workflows. Sustainable SEO growth in YMYL industries requires a long-term authority-building strategy rather than short-term tactical wins.
