# Kith and Kin

_Real Estate · Kuala Lumpur & Selangor_

**How a real estate agency increased lead volume by 392%**

## Overview

Kith and Kin is a reputable real estate agency specialising in development projects across Kuala Lumpur and Selangor, with a primary focus on a newly launched development in Dutamas, near the Mont Kiara district, targeting both local and expatriate buyers.

## Challenge

Before working with Toggle, Kith and Kin relied solely on organic methods and traditional cold-calling to generate leads, with no paid advertising presence. Toggle's objective was to increase qualified lead volume while keeping CPL at a sustainable level.

## Solution

Upon analysing the market landscape, Toggle identified a significant gap in paid advertising from competitors around the Dutamas area. The approach primarily focused on Meta Ads, deploying targeted lead generation and message-specific campaigns. A series of compelling ad creatives across gifs, images, and video formats effectively showcased the project's unique selling points while maintaining an optimal CPL.

**Services delivered:** Performance Marketing · Creative Production · Reporting & Analysis · Conversion Optimisation

## Results

| Metric                                  | Result   |
| --------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Lead volume increase                    | +392%    |
| Conversion rate (leads to deals closed) | 10%      |
| Cost per lead                           | RM35     |
| Period                                  | 3 months |

## Key insight

A mix of lead generation and message-specific campaigns, combined with engaging ad formats (gifs, images, videos), helped capture attention and inform the audience at multiple touchpoints. Continuous refinement of messaging tailored to the target audience proved critical in enhancing campaign performance and lead quality.
