## Promotion rule

Move a hook to `prompts/canonical/` when:

1. It has shipped in a real client campaign.
2. It performed against a defined metric (CTR, CPL, ROAS, completion rate).
3. The attribution is recorded (client, campaign, period, metric).

Until then, hooks live here as drafts.
