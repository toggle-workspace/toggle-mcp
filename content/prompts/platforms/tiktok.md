# TikTok prompt scaffold

Platform-specific prompt patterns for TikTok. Reference `brain/voice/tone-by-channel.md` for voice; this file holds format + hook patterns.

---

## Format rules

- Aspect: 9:16 vertical.
- Length: 15–60 seconds. Shorter = higher completion rate; longer = more context.
- Captions: burned-in, large enough for muted viewing. Auto-captions are insufficient.
- CTA placement: hook in first 1 second; CTA verbally + on-screen in last 3 seconds.
- Native > polished: production value should feel platform-native, not ad-shaped.

---

## Hook patterns

1. **Curiosity gap** — "Most people don't know that…"
2. **Contrarian** — "Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong."
3. **Stat hook** — "73% of [audience] fail at this. Here's the fix."
4. **Demo cold open** — show the result in frame 1, explain after.
5. **Negation** — "Don't do X if you want Y."
6. **Question hook** — "What if [scenario]?"
7. **POV hook** — "POV: you're trying to [outcome]."
8. **Pattern interrupt** — visual / audio cut that breaks scroll inertia.

Example lines:

- "If you're [audience], stop scrolling — this saves you [outcome]."
- "Three things I wish I knew before [decision]."

---

## CTA library

- "Comment [X] and I'll DM you [resource]."
- "Save this for when you [trigger]."
- "Link in bio — but only if you're serious about [outcome]."
- "Try it for a week and tell me what happened."

---

## What works

- Faces (real ones). Algorithm rewards human presence.
- Tight cuts (every 1–2s in the first 5 seconds).
- Native captions, native sound. Avoid voiceover-only.
- Repeated formats with topical variation.

---

## What doesn't

- Logo-first openings. Burns the hook window.
- Stock footage cutdowns.
- Hard sales pitch in the hook.
- Disabling sound (TikTok is sound-on by default).
