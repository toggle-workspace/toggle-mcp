# Meta (Facebook + Instagram) prompt scaffold

Platform-specific prompt patterns for Meta Ads. Reference `brain/voice/tone-by-channel.md` for voice.

---

## Format variants

| Placement | Aspect | Asset | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Feed | 1:1 or 4:5 | static / video | Primary text first 125 chars matter most |
| Reels | 9:16 | video | Sound-on, captions burned-in |
| Stories | 9:16 | static / video | Sticker-friendly; vertical-first |
| Right column (desktop) | 1.91:1 | static | Low priority — secondary placement |
| Marketplace | 1:1 | static | Avoid heavy text overlay |

---

## Ad copy structure

1. **Primary text** — first 125 chars carry the weight. Lead with the problem or hook.
2. **Headline** — 40 chars max. State the offer or value prop.
3. **Description** — secondary support. Often clipped — don't put critical info here.
4. **CTA button** — choose from Meta's native list (Shop Now, Learn More, Sign Up, etc.).

---

## Primary text patterns

- **Problem → solution:** "Tired of [problem]? Here's how [solution]."
- **Stat → claim:** "[stat]. That's why we built [product]."
- **Story open:** "Two years ago, [customer] was [state]. Today they're [outcome]."
- **Direct offer:** "[Discount / offer] on [product]. Ends [date]."

---

## CTA library

- "Shop Now" — bottom of funnel, e-comm.
- "Learn More" — top/middle of funnel, info-rich landing.
- "Sign Up" — lead gen, gated content.
- "Get Quote" — high-consideration B2B / services.
- "Book Now" — appointment / consultation.
- "Apply Now" — education, financial products.

---

## What works

- One clear value prop per ad.
- Native, lifestyle imagery. Stock cliché kills CTR.
- Carousels for multi-product or multi-benefit ads.
- Video < 15s for cold; 15–30s for warm.
- Captions burned-in on all video.

---

## What doesn't

- Wall-of-text primary text.
- Generic stock photography.
- Multiple CTAs in one ad.
- Mismatched landing page (ad promises X, page shows Y).
