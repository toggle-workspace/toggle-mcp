Lead Form Strategy

Conditional Meta Instant Forms: qualify the lead inside the ad, save sales time downstream.

Instant Form — Question Logic
Q1 — Which professional body are you interested in?
ACCA · ICAEW · I'm not sure yet
Q2 — What is your current status?
Working Professional · Fresh Graduate · Final-Year Student
Q3 — Preferred study mode?
100% Online (Flexible) · Blended · On-Campus (Malaysia)
Q4 — Which level are you starting at?
Professional Level · Foundation (FIA / CFAB) · Subject-specific
Auto-filled
Full Name · Email · Phone · City / Country
Why conditional logic wins
01
Lead quality score before sales sees it
Every lead arrives in CRM pre-tagged by qualification, status, mode, and level. Sales prioritises Working Professional + Professional Level first.
02
Routing without manual triage
ACCA Professional Level leads → senior advisor. Foundation leads → onboarding specialist. "Not sure yet" → nurture sequence.
03
Drop-off telemetry feeds creative
Form abandonment by question tells us which message-audience pairing is mismatched. We rewrite the creative or the targeting, not just the form.
04
Conversions API marries it to outcomes
Each form fill is matched back through CRM enrolment events, training Meta's algorithm to find leads that actually pay tuition — not just submit forms.
