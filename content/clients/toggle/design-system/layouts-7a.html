UNITAR · Higher-Ed

From Moonshot to Milestone

A leading Malaysian private university drowning in acquisition costs with no ceiling in sight. Three years later the funnel runs on enrolment data.

32,000+

The brief arrived as "we need more ads." The diagnosis said otherwise: leads were dying between form submit and first call, and the ad platforms were optimising toward whoever filled forms fastest — not whoever enrolled.

The engagement, in three moves

  1. ChallengeAcquisition costs with no ceiling

    CPL climbing quarter over quarter; channel mix locked on volume; no enrolment signal reaching the auction.

  2. SolutionRebuild the funnel, not the ads

    Conditional lead forms qualify inside the ad. CRM scores and routes. Outcome events fire back through Conversions API — the lead-quality loop.

  3. Result32,000+ leads at −47% CPL

    Optimised to enrolment from week 6 — and compounding across three intakes a year since.

What compounds now

Every intake inherits the last one's data: creative libraries, qualified-lead scoring, enrolment-trained audiences. The system gets cheaper to run each cycle — the opposite of the treadmill it replaced.
