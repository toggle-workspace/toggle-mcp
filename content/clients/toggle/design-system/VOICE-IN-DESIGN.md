# Voice in Design — the gut-check 1-pager (Stage 11)

> Pin this next to your screen. Before any Toggle asset ships — slide, post, page, ad — walk the five rows. If a design decision doesn't survive its row, change the design, not the rule. (Canon: BRAND-IDENTITY §6; devices: SIGNATURE-DEVICES; colors/type: TOKENS.)

| Voice | The binding design rule | The 5-second check |
|---|---|---|
| **Direct** | One CTA per surface; one decision per screen. | Can a stranger name the next action in under 3 seconds? Is there exactly one primary button? |
| **Honest** | The strikethrough rewrite is the signature device — strike only a REAL category default, **one per artifact**. Never render projections styled as data; non-actuals carry `[ TARGET ]` tags; disqualifiers cost us something real. | Is anything here flattering us? Would the claim survive the client reading it next to their own data? |
| **Sharp** | Numerals at display weight, tabular, static — specific over vague. One blue focal number per surface; everything else ink. | Does the number land before the words? Is it exact ("RM 154") rather than rounded marketing ("RM 150+")? |
| **Experienced** | Every assertion carries its receipt inline — stat beside claim, client + year beside stat, named operator beside expertise. No "trust us" copy, no proof in appendices. | Point at each claim. Is its evidence inside the same visual unit? |
| **Unpretentious** | No element exists purely for decoration; if it isn't load-bearing, delete it. Architectural-drawing discipline — every line means something. | Say each element's job out loud. Anything you can't justify, cut before shipping. |

**The two standing tests** (run last): **Tone** — show 3 outsiders for 5 s: do ≥2 say "engineering firm that does growth" not "B2B startup site"? **Swap** — could this ship from Linear/Vercel/Stripe with the logo changed? If yes, it isn't Toggle yet.

**Device budget per surface:** one isometric composition · one strikethrough (per ARTIFACT) · two seals · one focal blue stat · one toggle-flip. Most surfaces use one device, not all. When in doubt, subtract.
