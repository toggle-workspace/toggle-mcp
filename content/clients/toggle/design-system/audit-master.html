The three findings that matter

2 · The auction is optimising to form-fillers, not buyers

Evidence: [SAMPLE] zero outcome events returning from CRM; platform optimises on submits only.
Fix: Conversions API events for qualified / enrolled / dead — the lead-quality loop, live from week 6.

3 · One landing page serves four audiences

Evidence: [SAMPLE] 2.1% conversion blended; message match breaks for returning visitors.
Fix: persona-split pages with matched hooks — the messaging framework from our UNITAR rebuild (32,000+ leads · −47% CPL).
