# Stage 9 — Marketing Asset Library (9a–9c)

> LinkedIn-primary (Toggle's top-of-funnel), then Meta/Google, then utility. Artifact with true-dimension canvases at 30% preview: **`marketing-9.html`**. Production: render each canvas at 100% via headless Chrome screenshot (the wordmark-PNG pipeline) or rebuild in Canva/Figma from these specs.

## 9a — LinkedIn (primary channel)

**Stat card** — 1080×1080 + 1080×1350 · three variants: **single big stat** (kicker receipt → 300px numeral → ≤2-line consequence), **3-stat hero** (claim headline with ONE blue word → 3 stat cards, one focal → receipt line), **before/after** (two numerals, gray→blue, one-line verdict). Rules: wordmark bottom-left (the LinkedIn-capture canon) · seal bottom-right · one focal blue per card · receipt always inline (client · period) · no emoji, no headshots, no gradients · numerals tabular.
**Carousel** — 10 slides, 1080×1080: hook (the claim + its sharpest number) → 2 problem slides → 3 insight slides (the method, one move each) → proof (the −47%) → micro case → **the catch** (an honest disqualifier — slide 9 is the credibility slide) → CTA (the diagnostic ask + domain). Page numbers + wordmark every slide. Copy in the artifact is real (UNITAR/lead-quality-loop story).
**Belief:** *"This firm publishes its working data — following them is free consulting."*

## 9b — Meta + Google ad creative

Matrix: **3 sizes** (1080×1080, 1080×1350, 1200×628) × **3 hook formats** (stat-led, quote-led, before/after) = 9 assets per campaign burst; the artifact renders one representative per hook. Rules: one hook per asset · receipt inline · CTA lives in the platform button, never baked into the image · quote-led ships only with approved quotes (composite stays labeled) · maps to the Creative Strategy testing axes (hook × visual × proof — PROPOSAL-MASTER slide 11).
**Belief:** *"Even their ads show receipts."*

## 9c — Utility

**Email signature** (Viknesh / Zaid / generic — one template, name-role swap): wordmark 20px → name 700 → role · firm → phone + domain links in deep blue → tagline seal in muted gray. Table-based HTML, no images except the wordmark (attach as hosted PNG for mail clients), no banners.
**OG image** 1200×630: wordmark → positioning line → step-form right → domain seal. Favicon: unchanged (Stage 1 — current wordmark tile holds for v1). Twitter/X card uses the OG.
**Business card**: 90×54mm, **3mm bleed, 6mm safe area**, CMYK from TOKENS §1.9 (vendor-proof mandatory — the navy is the risk). Front dark: wordmark + name/role + mobile/domain. Back light: step-form + tagline seal. Stock note: uncoated 350gsm reads most "drafting paper" — confirm with vendor.
