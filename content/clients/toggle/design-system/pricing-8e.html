How the number gets set (the anchor logic — stated before any price)

  • Scoped to outcomes, not headcount: the fee tracks the revenue system under management (channels × markets × funnel depth), never hours.
  • The audit de-risks the retainer: the fixed-fee audit prices first and smallest; its roadmap defines the partnership scope — the first invoice buys clarity.
  • Media is never inside the fee: ad spend is the client's, billed direct to platform — the proposal always shows fee vs media separately (Stage 2 Commercials rule).
  • Anchored to the nearest two quotes: every quote cites the two most recent comparable engagements from the quote ledger (archive/quotes/) — pricing drifts on evidence, not mood.
