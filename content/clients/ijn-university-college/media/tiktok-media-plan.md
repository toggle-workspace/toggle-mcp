# IJNUC — TikTok Media Plan (RM10k/month)

**Client:** IJN University College (IJNUC), Malaysia
**Channel:** TikTok only
**Budget:** RM10,000/month (RM8k ad spend + RM2k management)
**Quote ref:** QT-00034, dated 05/06/2026, NET30
**Hard deadline:** 31 July registration cut-off

> Scope note: granola from the IJN meeting proposed RM3k TikTok / RM5k Meta. This plan assumes the issued quotation (TikTok-only at RM8k spend) is the operating scope. If Meta runs in parallel via another vendor, this plan stands unchanged.

## Objective

Drive RSVPs for the rotating weekend open days (5–7 of each month across 25 locations) and walk-ins to the daily KL HQ open day at Institut Jantung Negara lobby.

## KPI targets

Benchmark to beat (previous campaign): 205 RSVPs / 120 arrivals (58% show) / 38 apps / 70 enrolled at ~RM13k total spend.

TikTok-side targets for this plan:
- **≥150 RSVPs/month** at **≤RM50 CPL**
- **≥55% show rate** on RSVPs that came via TikTok
- Feed Apply Radar (Google ads partner) for cross-channel attribution

## Budget

| Line | Amount |
|---|---|
| Ad spend | RM 8,000 |
| Management | RM 2,000 |
| **Total** | **RM 10,000** |

Free creative supply to unlock before launch:
- **10 free TikTok One creator videos/week** (~40/month) — unlocked at 10k+ MYR monthly spend, which this plan meets

## Campaign structure

Single Lead Generation campaign, instant TikTok lead form.

Lead form fields (per IJN spec):
- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Preferred date + time
- Preferred location
- Program interest

Ad sets split by region; weights flip every Friday based on that weekend's event:

| Ad set | Default week | Event weekend |
|---|---|---|
| Klang Valley (always-on, KL HQ daily) | 35% | 25% |
| That weekend's host region(s) | — | 50% |
| Other regions (broad awareness) | 65% | 25% |

Backup ad set: WhatsApp click-to-message for high-intent users, cap at 10% of budget.

## Targeting

- **Geo:** 30km radius around event venue (50km in smaller cities); always-on KL HQ
- **Age:** 16–22 (primary), 35–55 in same geos (parents)
- **Interests:** healthcare careers, nursing, allied health, university applications, PTPTN
- **Language:** BM + English

## Creative — TikTok One (~40 videos/month)

Four angles rotated weekly so no single angle burns out:

1. **"Career relief"** — allied health framed as a legit career, not a backup to becoming a doctor
2. **"Open day vlog"** — creator visits the upcoming weekend's location, films campus tour + financial aid booth
3. **"POV: lepas SPM I almost did matrik until..."** — discovery-style hook
4. **"Real talk: PTPTN + allied health"** — affordability + career outcomes, parent-friendly

Every video must include:
- Program name + MQA code
- That weekend's location + date overlay
- RSVP CTA matching the open day banner
- IJNUC logo end-frame

## Operating cadence (weekly)

- **Mon** — load new creator videos; set next weekend's geo + creative
- **Wed** — kill bottom 30% of ads by CPL; double down on winners
- **Fri AM** — final QA: weekend geo live, location/date overlay correct
- **Sun PM** — pull arrivals from event team into the Google Sheet; feed show-rate by region back into next week's weights

## Reporting

- **Daily:** roll-up into IJN's existing Google Sheet — spend, RSVPs, CPL by region, by creative
- **Weekly:** arrivals/RSVPs (show rate), apps/arrivals (conversion), spend pacing vs RM8k cap
