# IJNUC – Allied Health as a Real Career (Not a Backup)

## Project Name
IJN University College (IJNUC) – Allied Health Sciences Open Day Drive

## Project Description
IJN University College is the academic arm of Institut Jantung Negara (Malaysia's National Heart Institute). IJNUC is recruiting SPM/STPM leavers into its Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences programs ahead of the **31 July registration deadline**. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: 16–22-year-old Malaysians who want a real healthcare career without going through 7 years of medical school. Collaboration goal: drive RSVPs to upcoming IJNUC open days across Malaysia via the lead form linked in the ad.

## Product Description
IJNUC offers MQA-accredited diploma and degree programs in Allied Health Sciences (e.g. Nursing, Cardiac Care, Medical Imaging-adjacent fields) and Managerial Sciences, taught inside Malaysia's national heart hospital environment. Students train in real clinical settings on the IJN campus, with direct exposure to Malaysia's leading cardiac specialists. PTPTN-eligible. Open days run daily Mon–Fri 9am–4pm at the IJN HQ lobby (145 Jln Tun Razak, KL) plus rotating weekend events across 25 locations nationwide.

## Product Selling Points
- A real healthcare career **without doing 7 years of med school** — allied health graduates work alongside doctors, not under them.
- **Trained inside Malaysia's National Heart Institute** — not a generic college, but the actual hospital where Malaysia's top cardiac care happens.
- **PTPTN-eligible**, MQA-accredited, with open days running across Malaysia so you can visit one near you.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** SPM/STPM leavers or early college students aged 17–22, based in Malaysia. Bonus if they once wanted med school but changed direction, or if they care about healthcare but haven't seen themselves in it yet.
- **Style:** Honest talking-head storytime in Manglish or BM. "I always thought…" / "I used to believe…" reveal structure.
- **Audience:** Malaysian Gen-Z (16–22) figuring out what to do after SPM/STPM, plus parents watching alongside.

## Video Suggestions
- **Duration:** 30–35s, 9:16.
- **Hook (first 2s):** "Korang ingat nak kerja kat hospital mesti jadi doktor? Tipu je tu." OR "POV: I thought healthcare = med school. I was wrong."
- **Visuals:** Creator walking through a hospital-style corridor or wearing scrubs/lab coat. Cut to creator pointing at on-screen text listing IJNUC's allied health programs. **Calendar overlay in second half:** full list of upcoming IJNUC open days (date · city · venue) — IJN to supply the live calendar before shoot. End frame: IJNUC logo + tagline "Dreams Become Reality" + CTA "Click the button below — RSVP for the open day near you."
- **Audio:** Inspirational / uplifting track from TikTok's Commercial Music Library.
- **Compliance:** Without permission from all necessary rights holders, creators must not include copyrighted music in their videos. Creators may use tracks selected from the Commercial Music Library. No claims about salary, guaranteed jobs, or job placement rates. Include the MQA code for the program shown.

## Avoid
Voiceover-only, slideshow, corporate narrator, "shape your future" clichés, salary or placement claims, comparisons to named universities (e.g. "better than UM/USM"), generic "education is important" openers, scrubs that imply the creator is a real practising clinician.
