# IJNUC – Real Talk: PTPTN + Allied Health (Parent-Friendly)

## Project Name
IJN University College (IJNUC) – Affordability & Parent-Trust Drive

## Project Description
IJN University College is the academic arm of Institut Jantung Negara (Malaysia's National Heart Institute). IJNUC is recruiting SPM/STPM leavers into its Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences programs ahead of the **31 July registration deadline**. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: parents aged 35–55 in Malaysia weighing whether to send their child into healthcare, plus 18–22-year-olds showing this video to their parents. Collaboration goal: drive RSVPs to upcoming IJNUC open days across Malaysia via the lead form linked in the ad.

## Product Description
IJNUC offers MQA-accredited diploma and degree programs in Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences, taught inside Malaysia's National Heart Institute. **PTPTN-eligible**, with financial aid counsellors available at every open day. Open days run daily Mon–Fri 9am–4pm at IJN HQ KL plus rotating weekend events across 25 locations nationwide.

## Product Selling Points
- **PTPTN-eligible** — government student financing covers the programs, so no big upfront cash needed.
- **Trained at Malaysia's National Heart Institute** — a credential parents recognise, not a "random college nobody's heard of."
- **Financial aid help on the spot** at every open day — parents can ask questions in person, no salesperson pressure.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** Older creator (22–35), or a parent-aged Malaysian creator (35–55), who can speak parent-to-parent. Calm, trustworthy tone. Manglish, BM, or BM-dominant.
- **Style:** "Real talk" / kitchen-table conversation. Direct address to camera. No costumes, no cosplaying scrubs.
- **Audience:** Malaysian parents 35–55 evaluating their child's options, and Gen-Z (18–22) who'll share the video with their parents.

## Video Suggestions
- **Duration:** 30–40s, 9:16.
- **Hook (first 2s):** "Anak korang nak masuk allied health? Ini yang korang patut tahu pasal PTPTN." OR "Real talk for parents — sebelum korang kata 'jangan, mahal sangat', tengok ni dulu."
- **Visuals:** Creator at kitchen table or living room, calm conversational tone. Clean on-screen text overlays (no chaotic edits): "PTPTN-eligible", "MQA-accredited", "Trained at IJN". Avoid showing specific fee numbers unless IJN provides verified figures. **Calendar overlay in second half:** full list of upcoming IJNUC open days (date · city · venue) — IJN to supply live calendar before shoot, so parents can plan to attend. End frame: IJNUC logo + "Dreams Become Reality" + CTA "Click the button below — bring your child to the nearest open day."
- **Audio:** Warm, conversational, non-distracting track from TikTok's Commercial Music Library. No dramatic music.
- **Compliance:** Without permission from all necessary rights holders, creators must not include copyrighted music in their videos. Creators may use tracks selected from the Commercial Music Library. No claims about salary, guaranteed jobs, scholarship eligibility, or specific PTPTN amounts without IJN's written confirmation. Include the MQA code for the relevant program.

## Avoid
Acting like a doctor or nurse, wearing scrubs, scaremongering ("don't waste your money on other colleges"), comparisons to named universities, salary or placement claims, "investment in your future" clichés, voiceover-only, fast-cut edits that look like a sales ad.
