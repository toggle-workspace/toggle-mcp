# IJNUC – Open Day Vlog (Creator Visits the Campus)

## Project Name
IJN University College (IJNUC) – Open Day Vlog Drive

## Project Description
IJN University College is the academic arm of Institut Jantung Negara (Malaysia's National Heart Institute). IJNUC is recruiting SPM/STPM leavers into its Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences programs ahead of the **31 July registration deadline**. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: 16–22-year-old Malaysians considering allied health or managerial careers, plus parents. Collaboration goal: drive RSVPs to upcoming IJNUC open days across Malaysia via the lead form linked in the ad.

## Product Description
IJNUC's open day experience includes a campus tour (real hospital learning environment), 1-on-1 career consultation with academic staff in allied health and managerial sciences, and a financial aid booth (PTPTN + scholarships). Open days run daily Mon–Fri 9am–4pm at the IJN HQ lobby (145 Jln Tun Razak, KL) plus rotating weekend events across 25 locations nationwide.

## Product Selling Points
- **Walk through a real hospital learning environment**, not a tour of empty classrooms.
- **Sit down 1-on-1 with academic staff** about your specific career interest — same day, no appointment needed.
- **Financial aid help on the spot** — PTPTN guidance and scholarship info handled at the venue.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** Lifestyle / vlog-style creators aged 18–22, comfortable filming themselves in public spaces. Should live near, or be willing to travel to, one of the upcoming open day locations.
- **Style:** First-person POV vlog. "Come with me to…" energy. Manglish or BM.
- **Audience:** Malaysian Gen-Z (16–22) who want to "see for themselves" before committing — high-intent, research-stage viewers.

## Video Suggestions
- **Duration:** 30–40s, 9:16.
- **Hook (first 2s):** "Come with me to IJNUC's open day — let me show you what it actually looks like." OR "I went to an open day inside a real hospital. Here's what happened."
- **Visuals:** First-person POV walking up to the venue entrance (sign visible). Quick cuts of: campus tour, the career consultation chat, the financial aid table, the QR code being scanned. Show creator's face reacting honestly. **Calendar overlay in second half:** full list of upcoming IJNUC open days (date · city · venue) — IJN to supply the live calendar before shoot, so viewers in other states see "your open day is on…". End frame: IJNUC logo + "EXPLORE. EXPERIENCE. GET INSPIRED." + CTA "Click the button below — RSVP for your nearest open day."
- **Audio:** Upbeat lo-fi / vlog-aesthetic track from TikTok's Commercial Music Library.
- **Compliance:** Without permission from all necessary rights holders, creators must not include copyrighted music in their videos. Creators may use tracks selected from the Commercial Music Library. Filming inside IJN HQ requires venue permission — IJN team to coordinate access. No claims about salary, guaranteed jobs, or scholarship outcomes.

## Avoid
Staged or overly-produced shots that look like a corporate video, voiceover-only narration over stock footage, faking attendance (creator must actually attend an open day), generic "university life" b-roll, comparisons to named universities, capturing identifiable patients or staff without consent.
