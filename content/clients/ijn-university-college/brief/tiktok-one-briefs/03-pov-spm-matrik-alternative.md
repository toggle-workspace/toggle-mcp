# IJNUC – POV: Lepas SPM I Almost Did Matrik

## Project Name
IJN University College (IJNUC) – Post-SPM Discovery Drive

## Project Description
IJN University College is the academic arm of Institut Jantung Negara (Malaysia's National Heart Institute). IJNUC is recruiting SPM/STPM leavers into its Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences programs ahead of the **31 July registration deadline**. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: 16–19-year-old Malaysians fresh out of SPM who are about to take the default path (matriculation / foundation / "boleh tunggu next intake"). Collaboration goal: drive RSVPs to upcoming IJNUC open days across Malaysia via the lead form linked in the ad.

## Product Description
IJNUC offers MQA-accredited diploma and degree programs that SPM leavers can enter directly — no matriculation, no foundation year required for eligible candidates. Programs cover Allied Health Sciences and Managerial Sciences, taught at Malaysia's National Heart Institute. PTPTN-eligible. Open days run daily Mon–Fri 9am–4pm at IJN HQ KL plus rotating weekend events across 25 locations nationwide.

## Product Selling Points
- **Skip the long detour** — eligible SPM leavers can enter directly without matrik or foundation.
- **Real hospital learning environment** at Malaysia's National Heart Institute, not a generic college campus.
- **Multiple open days across Malaysia** — pick a date and location near you, walk in, ask anything.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** SPM leavers or first-year college students aged 17–19, based in Malaysia. Should sound like they're talking to a friend, not a camera.
- **Style:** "POV / storytime" hook → discovery → cut to open day. Casual Manglish or BM. Filmed in a bedroom, mamak, or kitchen — relatable, not produced.
- **Audience:** Malaysian SPM leavers (16–19) and their friend groups, who default to matriculation or "tunggu dulu" without realising there are direct-entry options.

## Video Suggestions
- **Duration:** 25–35s, 9:16.
- **Hook (first 2s):** "POV: aku dah register matrik then my cousin showed me this." OR "POV: everyone said tunggu next intake. I didn't."
- **Visuals:** Start with creator at home/bedroom holding phone, looking unsure. Quick scroll-through of the IJNUC site/banner on phone screen. Cut to creator visibly at an open day (campus tour b-roll, QR code scan). **Calendar overlay in second half:** full list of upcoming IJNUC open days (date · city · venue) — IJN to supply live calendar before shoot. End frame: IJNUC logo + "EXPLORE. EXPERIENCE. GET INSPIRED." + CTA "Click the button below — find the open day near you."
- **Audio:** Trending POV-style track from TikTok's Commercial Music Library.
- **Compliance:** Without permission from all necessary rights holders, creators must not include copyrighted music in their videos. Creators may use tracks selected from the Commercial Music Library. No claims about acceptance rates, guaranteed scholarships, or being "easier" than matriculation. Include the MQA code for the relevant program.

## Avoid
Knocking matriculation or foundation programs directly (no "matrik is a waste"), corporate-looking edits, voiceover-only, slideshow, comparisons to named universities or competitor colleges, claims about how fast someone "gets a job after."
