# Postgraduate Diploma in Education — TikTok One creator brief

## 1. Hook & message

"If you have a degree and you've always wanted to teach, watch this."

## 2. Product / program

**Postgraduate Diploma in Education (UNITAR)** — a 1-year, fully online programme that turns any degree holder into a qualified teacher.

## 3. USPs

- Become a qualified teacher in 1 year — you don't have to quit your current job.
- Any bachelor's degree is accepted — your degree doesn't need to be in education.
- Start any month of the year — three intakes (January, May, September), study from anywhere in Malaysia.

## 4. Creator vibe

An office worker in their late 20s who has wanted to teach for years. Honest, conversational, talking straight to camera like they're telling a friend why they're making the change. Slightly tired in a real way (just got home from work), not polished. Manglish or English-led, never staged.

## 5. Scenes / structure

1. **Scene 1 (0–2s, hook):** Creator at their kitchen table, laptop open, still in office wear. Looks straight to camera. On-screen text: "I have a degree. I've always wanted to teach."
2. **Scene 2 (2–5s, payoff):** Quick cut — creator's finger taps the UNITAR programme page on the laptop screen. On-screen text lands fast: "1 year. Fully online. Any degree." The "you can do this without quitting your job" message must be clear by the end of this beat.
3. **Scene 3 (5–18s, build):** Creator talking to camera — why they're switching, what they wish they'd done at 25.
4. **Scene 4 (18–27s, build):** Quick cuts of a real classroom, kids raising hands, creator imagining themselves there.
5. **Scene 5 (27–30s, CTA):** Back to creator, single line straight to camera, then end card with the CTA button.

## 6. CTA & deliverables

**CTA exact wording:** "Click the button below to learn more."
**Format:** 9:16
**Duration:** 30s
**Deliverables:** 1 main video + 1 alt take of Scene 1 (alternative hook line for A/B test).
