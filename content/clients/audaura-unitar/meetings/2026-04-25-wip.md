## 1. Sunway MBA Case Study

### Partnership Model
- Keypath (global OPM) handles online pedagogy, delivery, and marketing
- 50/50 revenue share — Sunway MBA at RM60k, Keypath takes RM30k clean
- Delivery costs are Keypath's responsibility; Sunway retains margin without building digital infrastructure

### Performance Metrics
- Average CPL: RM87
- 55% of leads contactable/validated (vs. UNITAR's ~80%)
- 75% conversion rate from contactable leads → enrollment
- 37% overall lead-to-enrollment conversion rate
- Very low attrition — higher pricing drives commitment and completion

### Scale
- 6 cycles per year, ~100 enrollments per cycle
- ~2,400 leads annually
- Master's Data Science: ~150 enrollments per cycle at RM150 CPL

### Key Takeaway
- Premium pricing filters intent — those who enroll complete. Higher price = higher perceived value = lower dropout
- Early mover advantage in Data Science was a key differentiator

## 3. UC vs. Main Campus Strategy

### Current Status
- UC budget allocation: 30–40% (target: 35%+)
- UC lead creation up 50%+ since March 1
- UC lead generation rate ~2x main campus
- Engagement-to-application rate: UC 13.6% vs. Main Campus 7.3%

### Strategic Direction
- Rationale for pushing UCs: less competitive market, existing investment in campuses, higher growth potential
- Algorithm currently favors KL due to population density and engagement — this is a natural market signal, not a forced allocation
- To push UC share beyond 35%: shift targeting so UC-area audiences are prioritized for UC campaigns, reducing KL dominance

### Growth Markets Identified
- **Terengganu / Kota Bharu** — large SPM pool, improving trust in UNITAR as quasi-government institution, participation in Jom Masuk U / MARA events creating halo effect
- **Kota Kinabalu (KK)** — significant untapped potential
- Overall applications up 80% YoY as of April 25 — caveat: SPM results released today last year, so gap will narrow; realistic expectation is ~30% up by end of intake window

## 4. Creative & UI Testing

### Color/UI Experiment (4 variations)
- **Variation 1:** Highest scalability, clicks, CTR, engagements — highest CPL but difference is marginal
- **Variation 4 (MBA):** Best scalability, clicks, CTR, CPC — highest leads
- **Conclusion:** Color scheme is not the core issue. Look, feel, and content presentation are. Functionality and information accessibility must take priority over branding aesthetics
