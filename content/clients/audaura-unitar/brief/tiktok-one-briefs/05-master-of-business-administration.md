# UNITAR – Master of Business Administration (Online MBA, RM 12k, EPF-Eligible)

## Project Name
UNITAR – Master of Business Administration – Online (UUCKL, RM 12k Total Fee)

## Project Description
UNITAR International University is recruiting working professionals into the Master of Business Administration – Online at its UUCKL campus in Kuala Lumpur. Non-physical product (postgraduate education programme). Target audience: 26–40-year-old Malaysian working professionals — executives, managers, entrepreneurs, mid-career staff in any industry — who already have a bachelor's degree and want an MBA to move into senior management, switch industries, or run their own business, without quitting their job. Collaboration goal: drive enquiries to UNITAR via unitar.my/request-info.

## Product Description
A 3-semester MQA-accredited Master of Business Administration delivered fully online by UNITAR University College Kuala Lumpur (UUCKL). Designed for working professionals — study from anywhere on your own schedule. Covers finance, marketing, operations, HR, strategy, analytics, and digital business, with international case studies and exposure to industry networking. EPF (KWSP) withdrawal is approved AND it qualifies for personal income tax relief, so it can be funded without out-of-pocket cash. Total tuition from **RM 12,000** (down from RM 18,462).

## Product Selling Points
- **Full MBA for RM 12,000 total** — one of the most affordable MQA-accredited MBAs in Malaysia, with EPF (KWSP) withdrawal AND personal income tax relief approved.
- **Finish in 3 semesters, 100% online** — study after work, on weekends, on your phone if you have to. No need to quit your job or move cities.
- **Real business skills, not just theory** — finance, marketing, operations, strategy, analytics, digital tools, plus networking access to business leaders and case studies from real companies.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** Malaysian working professionals aged 26–38 — executives, managers, sales/marketing leads, HR / ops staff, accountants, engineers, founders of small businesses, family-business successors. Already hold a bachelor's degree. Mix of male and female.
- **Style:** "Day in my life as a manager/exec", "What I'm doing after work to level up", "How I'm funding my Master's with KWSP" — honest talking-head storytime in BM, Manglish, or English. Calm, sincere, slightly aspirational, not over-produced. Office wear or smart-casual.
- **Audience:** Malaysian working professionals (26–40) feeling stuck at their current level, fresh degree grads with 2–4 years of work experience wondering what's next, and small-business owners who want to professionalise their company.

## Avoid
Voiceover-only, slideshow, corporate narrator, "boss life" / "CEO mindset" / "invest in yourself" clichés, specific salary or promotion claims, guaranteed-job or guaranteed-raise language, comparisons to named universities or named MBA programmes, "the world is changing fast" generic openers, implying the MBA automatically makes you a manager or doubles your pay, flashing luxury cars / watches / cash, and any wording that frames EPF withdrawal as "free money."
