# UNITAR – Diploma in Culinary Arts (Hands-On Chef Training)

## Project Name
UNITAR – Diploma in Culinary Arts (Real Kitchen, Real Chef Career)

## Project Description
UNITAR College Kota Kinabalu is recruiting SPM leavers and aspiring chefs into the Diploma in Culinary Arts — a 2-year, hands-on programme that trains students to become professional chefs, catering managers, or F&B business owners. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: 17–24-year-olds in Sabah / East Malaysia who love cooking, plus food-content viewers nationally. Collaboration goal: drive enquiries to wasap.my/60163309999 or unitar.my/request-info.

## Product Description
A 2-year MQA-accredited culinary diploma (R2/1013/4/0121 · A11469) at UNITAR College Kota Kinabalu. Students cook every week in a real training kitchen — basic & intermediate pastry, Malaysian cuisine, Western cuisine, international cuisine, food sanitation, kitchen organisation, costing & menu management — plus Industrial Training in a real F&B business. PTPTN-eligible. Campus is in Alamesra, Kota Kinabalu, Sabah.

## Product Selling Points
- **Real kitchen training, not classroom slides** — you cook every week: pastry, Malaysian, Western & international cuisine, plus plating.
- **Get in with just 3 SPM credits** — no foundation, matriculation or STPM needed.
- **Built for real F&B careers** — graduates become chefs, catering managers, or start their own food business; includes Industrial Training placement.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** Food lovers, aspiring chefs, culinary students or home cooks aged 17–28, Sabah / East Malaysia preferred (campus is in Kota Kinabalu); KL-based food creators acceptable.
- **Style:** Cooking POV / "what I cooked today" / "day in the life of a culinary student" — satisfying knife work, plating shots, ASMR cooking sounds, talking-head reveal.
- **Audience:** Malaysian Gen-Z food fans, home cooks, and SPM leavers (and their parents) deciding what to do after results.
