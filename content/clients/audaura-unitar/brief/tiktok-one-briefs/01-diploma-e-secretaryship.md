# UNITAR – Diploma in E-Secretaryship (Office & PA Career)

## Project Name
UNITAR – Diploma in E-Secretaryship (Office & PA Career Pathway)

## Project Description
UNITAR is a Malaysian private university group recruiting SPM leavers into the Diploma in E-Secretaryship — a 2-year office administration & personal-assistant qualification. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: 17–22-year-old Malaysians who finished SPM and want a real career without doing matriculation or a full degree. Collaboration goal: drive enquiries to UNITAR via wasap.my/60163309999 or unitar.my/request-info.

## Product Description
A 2-year MQA-accredited diploma at UNITAR College that trains students to become Secretaries, Personal Assistants, and Office Administrators. Students learn the actual tools used in real offices — typing & document processing, MS Office, business writing, records management, basic accounting — plus an On-the-Job Training placement. Available at UNITAR College campuses across Malaysia (Sungai Petani, Penang, Ipoh, JB, Kuantan, KT, Kota Bharu, KK). PTPTN-eligible.

## Product Selling Points
- Get into a real office career with just **3 SPM credits** — no STPM, foundation or matriculation needed.
- **Real office skills, not just theory** — typing, MS Office, business writing, document handling, plus an On-the-Job Training placement.
- **Affordable + PTPTN-eligible** with low registration fees and 8 UNITAR College campuses across Malaysia.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** SPM leavers / college students aged 17–24, female-skewing, based in Malaysia. Bonus if they have a "corporate girl" / office-aesthetic vibe.
- **Style:** "Day in my life" / "GRWM for the office" / honest talking-head storytime in Manglish or BM.
- **Audience:** Malaysian Gen-Z (16–22) figuring out what to do after SPM, plus parents watching alongside.
