# UNITAR – Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Become a Teacher in 1 Year, Online)

## Project Name
UNITAR – Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Career Switch into Teaching)

## Project Description
UNITAR International University is recruiting degree holders into the Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE) — a 1-year, fully online qualification that prepares them to teach in preschool, primary, secondary or tertiary settings. Non-physical product (education programme). Target audience: working adults aged 25–40 with a bachelor's degree who want to switch into teaching without quitting their current job. Collaboration goal: drive enquiries to wasap.my/60163309999 or unitar.my/request-info.

## Product Description
A 1-year MQA-accredited postgraduate qualification (R/0111/7/0003 · MQA/FA 4929) delivered fully online (Open & Distance Learning) by UNITAR International University. Covers teaching methodology, psychology of learning, curriculum design, classroom management, and a teaching practicum. Three intakes a year — January, May, September. Graduates can also credit-transfer into UNITAR's Master of Education.

## Product Selling Points
- **Become a qualified teacher in 1 year** — fully online, so you don't need to quit your current job.
- **Any bachelor's degree is accepted** — even if your degree isn't in education, you can still switch into teaching.
- **Start anytime** — three intakes per year (January, May, September), study from anywhere in Malaysia.

## Preferred Creator Description
- **Identity:** Working adults aged 25–40 with a bachelor's degree — especially career-switchers, mums returning to work, or office workers feeling unfulfilled.
- **Style:** Honest, conversational talking-head — "why I'm leaving my office job", "what I wish I knew at 25", "my career-switch journey" — Manglish or English-led.
- **Audience:** Malaysians 25–40 thinking about a career pivot, particularly toward teaching, parenting-friendly work, or more meaningful careers.
