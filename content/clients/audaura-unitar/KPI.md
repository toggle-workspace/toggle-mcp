# UNITAR — KPI definition

## The operative KPI

**Net-lead gap per campus.** Defined as:

> _For each of the 10 UNITAR campuses, the gap between net leads delivered and
> the campus's 2026 C2 net-lead target. Tracked per campus, not blended._

The headline number Zaid reports up is the **sum of per-campus gaps still
outstanding**, with worst-three campuses called out by name.

## What the KPI is NOT

- **Not blended CPL.** Blended CPL is an explicit anti-goal — per-campus net
  lead gap is the only KPI that drives campus-level decisions.
- **Not gross lead count.** Net leads = de-duped, validated, post-quality-filter.
- **Not "leads per RM spent".** That's a campaign-tactical metric, not the
  client-level KPI.

## Why this matters

The C2 2026 strategy doc (`TG/C2 2026/.../C2 2026 UC Homegrown Media Strategy.md`)
mandates per-campus tracking because campus economics differ enough that
blending hides Johor (high CPL, high scalability) under cheap traffic from
SPM outstation BM.

## Anti-goals (binding)

- Reporting blended CPL as the headline.
- Sharing budget, audiences, or creatives across UC homegrown and
  Online/Selangor campaign trees.
- Mixing Online UIU into the UC homegrown campaign tree.
